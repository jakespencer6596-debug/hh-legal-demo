Local Loves: Our Top Picks for Food and Drink in St. Pete's

Feb 5 2021 · Community

We spend a lot of time downtown between courthouse trips and client meetings. Here are the spots that keep us going — none sponsored, all favorites.

Coffee

Bandit Coffee Co. on Central is our morning standby. Black Crow Coffee Co. for the patio. Driftwood for the pour-over.

Lunch

Engine No. 9 for a burger that isn't apologizing. Bodega for the Cuban. Red Mesa for tacos that hold up.

Dinner

The Library for date nights. Annata Wine Bar for the pasta and the room. Rococo Steak when somebody else is buying.

Cocktails

Stillwaters Tavern, easily. The Mandarin Hide if you want a quieter room and a real drinks list.

Sweet

Kahwa for affogato. The Hyppo for popsicles. Brick & Mortar for a kitchen finish that lingers.

The takeaway

If you live in Tampa Bay and we haven't run into you at one of these places yet, give it time.
